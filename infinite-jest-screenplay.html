A Screenplay Loosely Based on Infinite Jest

I wrote this for a half-term class I took at MIT, Big Books: Infinite Jest. On our last day of class, we did a reading. It was fantastic to see how each person in my class brought their own unique voice, bringing the text to life with a whole new personality I could not have imagined on my own. Enjoy!
